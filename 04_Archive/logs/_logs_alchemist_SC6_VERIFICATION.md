## E4: exa-search扩源证据

### SC6-2 Atomic Habits四法则验证

**来源1**: James Clear, Atomic Habits (2018)
**原文框架**:
- 1st Law (Cue): Make it obvious → 好习惯：让提示明显
- 2nd Law (Craving): Make it attractive → 好习惯：让渴望有吸引力
- 3rd Law (Response): Make it easy → 好习惯：让行动简单
- 4th Law (Reward): Make it satisfying → 好习惯：让奖励满足
**逆向应用（打破坏习惯）**:
- Inversion 1st: Make it invisible → 让坏习惯不可见
- Inversion 2nd: Make it unattractive → 让坏习惯无吸引力
- Inversion 3rd: Make it difficult → 让坏习惯困难
- Inversion 4th: Make it unsatisfying → 让坏习惯无满足感
**URL**: https://jamesclear.com/atomic-habits-summary

### SC6-2 环境设计科学研究

**来源**: James Clear, "Choice Architecture" (2014-01-27)
**核心研究**: Anne Thorndike, Massachusetts General Hospital cafeteria study:
- 改变饮料摆放位置（水放在各饮料站+收银台→汽水销售降11.4%，水销售增25.8%）
- 结论：环境是行为的无形之手。每个习惯都由环境线索触发。
**URL**: https://jamesclear.com/choice-architecture
**DOI**: Thorndike et al., American Journal of Public Health 102, no.3 (2012), doi:10.2105/ajph.2011.300391
**证据等级**: L2 (同行评审学术论文)

### SC6-5 身份驱动习惯理论

**来源**: Atomic Habits, Chapter 2
**核心论点**: "The ultimate form of intrinsic motivation is when a habit becomes part of your identity." 行为的最高形式内在动机是习惯成为身份的一部分。
另见: James Clear, "Identity-Based Habits: How to Actually Stick to Your Goals This Year" → "Your current behaviors are simply a reflection of your current identity."

### Dopamine Nation 补充（SC6负向飞轮）

**来源**: Anna Lembke, Dopamine Nation (2021)
**核心机制**: 手机=现代皮下注射针，24/7提供数字多巴胺。过度刺激→多巴胺基线下降→需要更多刺激才能感到正常→负循环。
**NPR访谈原文**: Lembke: "The smartphone is the modern-day hypodermic needle, delivering digital dopamine 24/7 for a wired generation."
**证据等级**: L2w (weread·161条高亮)
